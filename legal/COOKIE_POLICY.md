## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, to improve user experience, and to provide information to the website operator.

Cookies may be:
- **Session cookies** — temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser.
- **Persistent cookies** — cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them.
- **First-party cookies** — set by the website you are visiting.
- **Third-party cookies** — set by a third party (e.g., analytics provider).

## 3. Cookie Categories and Specific Cookies

### 3.1 Essential Cookies (Strictly Necessary)

These cookies are necessary for the operation of our service. They cannot be disabled without impairing core functionality. Consent is **not required** for essential cookies under Article 5(3) of the ePrivacy Directive.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Type | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|

| `subsnoop_csrf` | Cross-Site Request Forgery protection token | First-party, Session | Session |

**Legal basis:** Article 5(3)(b) ePrivacy Directive — strictly necessary for the provision of an explicitly requested service.

### 3.2 Analytics Cookies (Opt-In)

These cookies help us understand how users interact with our service. They are **only placed after you have given explicit consent** via our cookie consent banner.

| Cookie Name | Provider | Purpose | Type | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `_ph_*` | PostHog (EU Cloud) | Product analytics — page views, feature usage, session duration | First-party (via PostHog), Persistent | 13 months |
| `ph_*` | PostHog (EU Cloud) | User identity for analytics (pseudonymised) | First-party (via PostHog), Persistent | 13 months |

**Analytics configuration:**
- IP addresses are **anonymised** before storage.
- No cross-site tracking.

- Analytics data is **aggregated and anonymised** after 13 months.

**Legal basis:** Article 6(1)(a) GDPR — consent. You may withdraw consent at any time via the cookie settings panel in your account.
