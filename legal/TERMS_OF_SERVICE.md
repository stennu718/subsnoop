## 3. Service Description

SubSnoop is a PSD2-compliant subscription detection service that:

1. Connects to your bank account(s) via authorised Open Banking providers (Nordigen/GoCardless).
2. Analyses your transaction history to detect recurring payments and subscriptions.
3. Presents detected subscriptions in a dashboard for your review and management.
4. Provides insights into your subscription spending.

The Service is provided on an "as-is" and "as-available" basis. We do not guarantee 100% detection accuracy, as subscription detection depends on the quality and completeness of bank transaction data.
