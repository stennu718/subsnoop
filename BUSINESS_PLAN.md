## 1. Executive Summary

**SubSnoop** is an Estonian subscription intelligence platform that connects to bank accounts via PSD2 Open Banking, automatically detects all recurring subscriptions, and gives users a single dashboard to track, analyze, and optimize their spending.

**The Problem:** The average Estonian has 8-14 recurring subscriptions (Spotify, Netflix, gym, SaaS, telecom). Most underestimate their monthly burn by 30-40%.

**The Solution:** SubSnoop auto-detects subscriptions from bank transactions, shows total monthly cost, identifies unused subscriptions, and suggests savings.

**Why Now:**
- EU Open Banking (PSD2) is regulated and growing at 27% CAGR
- No local competitor in Estonia (TrueLayer, Mint are UK/US-focused)
- AI Act requires transparency — we're already compliant
