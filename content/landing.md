## FAQ

### Is it safe?

Yes. SubSnoop uses PSD2 Open Banking APIs to connect to your bank. This means we never see your login credentials — authentication happens directly with your bank. We have read-only access, so we can look at transactions but cannot move money, make payments, or change anything in your account. We are registered with the Estonian Financial Supervision Authority (Finantsinspektsioon) as an Account Information Service Provider (AISP). All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).

### Which banks do you support?

We support all Estonian banks that comply with PSD2:

- **Swedbank**
- **SEB**
- **LHV**
- **Luminor**
- **Coop Pank**
- **Inbank**

If your bank isn't listed, you can always upload a CSV export — it works with any bank, anywhere.

### How accurate is the detection?

Our detection engine identifies recurring charges with 96% precision. We classify each detection as **Confirmed** (high confidence — same merchant, same amount, regular interval), **Likely** (same merchant, slight amount variation or gap), or **Possible** (pattern match needing your review). You always have the final say — confirm or dismiss any detection with one click. False positives are rare (<4%) and get smarter over time as you correct them.

### Is it free?

Yes! The Free tier includes one bank connection, detection over the last 3 months, and up to 5 tracked subscriptions. For most people starting out, that's enough to find the forgotten subscriptions hurting their budget. Upgrade to Pro when you want unlimited tracking, 12-month history, and full spending analytics.

### What about GDPR?

You are in full control of your data. SubSnoop is GDPR-compliant by design:

- You can export all your data at any time (machine-readable JSON or CSV)
- You can permanently delete your account and all associated data with one click
- Data is stored on servers located in the EU (Frankfurt, Germany)
- We never sell, share, or monetise your financial data
- Our data processing agreement is published at /legal/dpa
- You can contact our DPO at dpo@subsnoop.ee
