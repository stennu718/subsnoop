## Key features (bullet list for articles)

- **Auto-detection engine** — scans up to 12 months of transaction history, identifies recurring charges with 96 % precision. Handles fixed amounts, variable amounts, irregular intervals, and merchants that rename themselves.
- **PSD2 bank connection** — read-only OAuth flow to every major Estonian bank (Swedbank, SEB, LHV, Luminor, Coop Pank, Inbank). Authentication happens at the bank — SubSnoop never sees credentials.
- **One dashboard** — every subscription in one place: cost, frequency, next billing date, usage health.
- **One-click cancellation** — direct links to the provider's cancellation page. Phone numbers, email templates, and "cancel by" reminders for providers without self-serve cancellation.
- **Spending analytics** — monthly/annual breakdown, category view (streaming, software, fitness, insurance, utilities), health score.
- **Privacy-first** — EU-hosted (Frankfurt), GDPR-compliant, registered AISP with Estonian Finantsinspektsioon. Export or delete all data at any time.
- **CSV fallback** — works with any bank in the world via CSV upload.
- **Referral programme** — both referrer and referred user get one month free on Pro.
