# Product Hunt Taglines

Three options, each under 60 characters. Pick one for the PH listing.

---

**Option 1 (48 chars):**
> Find and cancel forgotten subscriptions in 30 seconds

**Option 2 (52 chars):**
> Connect your bank. See every subscription. Cancel the waste.

**Option 3 (44 chars):**
> The subscription detector your bank forgot to build

---

## Recommendation

Use **Option 1** for the main PH tagline — it leads with the outcome (find + cancel), includes a credibility signal (30 seconds), and matches the hero message on the landing page for consistent branding.

Save Option 2 for the first sentence of the PH description. Keep Option 3 as a backup for Twitter/X posts and paid ads.
