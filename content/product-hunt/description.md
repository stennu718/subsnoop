# Product Hunt — First Comment

This is the first comment posted by the founder immediately after launch. It sets the tone, tells the story, and walks visitors through the product. Pin this to the top of the comments section.

---

**Hey Product Hunt! 👋**

I'm Sten, and I built SubSnoop after a personal wake-up call.

Last December I was reviewing my bank statement and noticed a €14.99 charge from a SaaS tool I signed up for during a free trial eight months earlier. I'd completely forgotten about it. So I dug deeper — and found three more forgotten subscriptions I was still paying for. Total: €47/month I didn't know I was spending.

I asked friends. Everyone had at least one. Most had several. One buddy was paying for two separate cloud storage services and a meditation app he opened exactly once.

**The problem:** Banks show you transactions, but they don't show you *subscriptions*. There's no "recurring charges" tab. No "you're paying for this every month" alert. You have to find them yourself — and most people don't.

**The solution:** SubSnoop connects to your bank via PSD2 (read-only, bank-grade security), scans up to 12 months of transactions, and automatically identifies every recurring charge. Then it gives you one dashboard to track them all and one-click cancellation links to kill the ones you don't need.

**Here's how it works (60-second demo):**

1. **Connect** — Click "Connect Bank", pick your bank, authenticate with your bank's own login (we never see your credentials). Takes 10 seconds.
2. **Scan** — We pull the last 12 months of transactions and run them through our detection engine. Takes 20 seconds.
3. **Review** — See every subscription we found, sorted by cost. Confirm or dismiss each one. Takes 20 seconds.
4. **Act** — Cancel anything you don't need with one click. We link you straight to the provider's cancellation page.

**What we found in beta:** 500+ Estonian users discovered an average of €47/month in forgotten subscriptions. That's €564/year — for stuff they weren't using.

**Pricing:** Free tier (1 bank, 3 months, 5 subs). Pro at €5.99/month (unlimited everything). Premium at €9.99/month (24-month history + annual reports).

We're launching in Estonia first — 620,000+ people here already use PSD2 banking apps, so the infrastructure is ready. We'll expand to Latvia and Lithuania in Q3, then broader Europe.

**Try it now:** [subsnoop.ee](https://subsnoop.ee) — no credit card needed for the free tier.

Would love your feedback, questions, and especially your honest critiques. What should we build next? 🚀
