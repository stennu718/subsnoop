Hey Product Hunt 👋,

I'm Sten — I built SubSnoop after finding a €14.99 charge from a free-trial SaaS tool I forgot about eight months earlier. Dug deeper. Found three more. **€47/month I didn't know I was spending.** It's everywhere: gym memberships since 2022, cloud storage you forgot, apps you opened once.

**SubSnoop connects to your bank via PSD2, finds every recurring charge across 12 months of history, and shows you one dashboard to track or cancel them all.** Read-only — we can never move your money.

Here's how it works:

1. **Connect** — click "Connect Bank", pick yours, you authenticate with your bank directly. We never see your credentials. *Takes 10 seconds.*
2. **Scan** — we pull up to 12 months of transactions, run the detection engine, and flag every recurring charge. Fixed, variable, irregular intervals, even merchants that name-shift. *Takes 20 seconds.*
3. **Review** — see every subscription sorted by cost. Confirm each one with one click. False positive rate: under 4%. *Takes 20 seconds.*
4. **Act** — one-tap cancellation links. We send you straight to the provider's cancellation page. No phone calls, no chatbots.

In beta, 500+ Estonian users found an **average of €47/month** in forgotten subscriptions. That's €564/year for stuff they weren't using.

**Pricing:**
- Free — 1 bank, 3-month history, up to 5 subscriptions
- Pro €5.99/mo — unlimited everything, 12-month history, spending analytics
- Premium €9.99/mo — 24-month history, annual reports

We're live in Estonia first (620k+ PSD2 banking users, infrastructure ready). Latvia & Lithuania next quarter, broader Europe after that.
